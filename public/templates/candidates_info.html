The Vote

The Nigerian presidential election is a significant event in Nigeria's democratic process, and the outcome will have significant implications for the country's future direction. This is where Nigerians will choose a new President for the country. Eligible voters must be registered and have a valid voter's card in order to vote in the presidential election.

Why I should cast my vote in the election?

1. This election will have repercussions. Each vote counts.

2. Voting provides a chance for change. Be the change you want to see.

3. You have the right. You lose your voice when you don't vote.

4. Nigeria's future is counting on you!
